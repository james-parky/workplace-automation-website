FYP Portal Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how our organisation uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

As of 30/03/2022 the FYP Portal exists as a Proof of Concept. As such, genuine personal and identifiable data should not be provided through any forms. Any data voluntarily submitted through the FYP Portal is not guaranteed to be secured as described below. Should this solution ever be deployed for use, the party deploying this solution or any variation of it should ensure the GDPR statement, procured and amended from here, below is enforced.

FYP Portal collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information as provided by UCL Azure Active Directory (Name, UCL email address)

  • Any and all information you enter through a form you submit through the FYP Portal.

You directly provide FYP Portal with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Forms you fill through the FYP Portal.

FYP Portal may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • UCL Active Directory

FYP Portal collects your data so that we can:

  • If you are a staff member, the data filled through the Supervisor Application will be shared with students who access this portal.

  • If you are a student, the data filled through the Student Application will be viewable to administrating staff of the FYP Module to find a suitable supervisor.

FYP Portal securely stores your data in Dataverse, secured by Microsoft.

FYP Portal will keep your Student Application data for 1 year. FYP Portal will keep your Supervisor Application data until you opt to not participate in the FYP Supervision scheme. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by permanently clearing the database tables in which your data is stored.

FYP Portal will not send you information about products and services of ours that we think you might like, nor those of our partner companies.
